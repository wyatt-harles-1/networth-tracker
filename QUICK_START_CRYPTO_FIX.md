# Quick Start: Fix Your Cryptocurrency Prices

## The Problem
Your Bitcoin shows at $47.14, but the actual market price is around $110,000. This affects all your cryptocurrency holdings.

## The Solution (3 Simple Steps)

### Step 1: Navigate to Assets Page
Open your portfolio application and go to the **Assets** page.

### Step 2: Click "Fix Crypto Prices"
Look for the button in the top-right corner of the page (next to the refresh icon).

### Step 3: Run the Correction
1. A dialog will open explaining what the tool does
2. Click **"Start Price Correction"**
3. Wait 1-2 minutes while it corrects your prices
4. Review the results (it will show old vs new prices)
5. Click **"Close and Refresh"** to see your updated portfolio
